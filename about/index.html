ABOUT

Samantha Shon is a second year undergraduate Mathematics of Computation student at the University of California, Los Angeles. As an Asian American, Samantha is part of the Pilipinos in Engineering and Sciences (PIES), a club dedicated to developing a community Filipino STEM students. Additionally, Samantha has been active in the UCLA women's robotics team, competing in the University Rover Competition, as a member of the programming subteam. Samantha also loves to design and sew her own clothes as she is part of Unravel, a sustainable fashion club, as a designer for their runway show. She also enjoys playing the piano as well as kick a soccer ball around!
